This morning, I went birding across the campus starting at sunrise. Below is the species list, and two ID shots- Ruby-crowned kinglet and Yellow warbler.

2 Canada Goose  
2 Mourning Dove  
1 Red-bellied Woodpecker  
1 Downy Woodpecker  
2 Eastern Phoebe  
2 Blue Jay  
2 American Crow  
1 Common Raven  
3 Black-capped Chickadee  
2 Tufted Titmouse  
1 House Wren  
2 Ruby-crowned Kinglet  
2 American Robin  
1 Northern Mockingbird  
1 Ovenbird  
1 Black-and-white Warbler  
1 Yellow Warbler  
1 Black-throated Blue Warbler  
1 Chipping Sparrow  
2 White-throated Sparrow  
1 Song Sparrow  
1 Northern Cardinal  
1 Common Grackle  
1 House Finch  
1 American Goldfinch  
2 House Sparrow

*1 Black-throated Green Warbler, *Yellow-rumped Warbler found later.

Number of Taxa: 26 + 2
